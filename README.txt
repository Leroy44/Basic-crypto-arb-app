Technologies: Python, Windows 10 OS 
Libraries: Pandas, Numpy, pathlib
Datasets: bitcoin price data from bitstamp and coinbase exchanges

The objective of the cryptocurrency arbitrageur for the purpose of the this study is to maximize bitcoin arbitrage profit by buying cheap on one exchange and selling higher on the other.  Such opportunities are rare, so it is important to study a reasonably high frequency of intraday price observations, which has been collected.  Secondly, it is important to prepare our data by cleaning out values that might contaminate the results of our analysis. This is a "collect --> prepare --> analyze" process for data analytics.
